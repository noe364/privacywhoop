Personal Use App

Privacy Policy

WHOOP Data Reporter  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Overview

WHOOP Data Reporter ("the App") is a personal automation tool built for private use only. It connects to the WHOOP API to retrieve health and fitness data and generates daily wellness reports. This App is not a commercial product and is not available to the general public.

2. Data Collected

The App accesses the following data from your WHOOP account, with your explicit authorization:

  • Recovery score and HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
  • Sleep data (duration, stages, sleep score)
  • Strain and workout data
  • Physiological cycle data

3. How Data Is Used

Your WHOOP data is used solely for the following purpose:

  • Generating a daily personal health and performance report
  • Providing personalized recommendations via an AI model (Claude by Anthropic)
  • Delivering the report to your personal email address

Your data is never sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.

4. Data Storage

This App does not maintain a database or persistent storage of your WHOOP data. Data is fetched in real time, processed to generate the report, and immediately discarded. No health data is stored on any server beyond the duration of a single automation run.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services solely to operate the automation:

  • WHOOP API – to retrieve your health data (developer.whoop.com)
  • Make.com – automation platform to schedule and run the workflow
  • Anthropic Claude API – to generate the daily report text
  • Gmail API – to deliver the report to your personal inbox

Each service operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service.

6. Authentication & Security

The App uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with your WHOOP account. Your WHOOP credentials are never stored or accessed by the App. Access tokens are managed securely by Make.com and are only used to retrieve your data during scheduled runs.

7. User Rights

As the sole user of this App, you may at any time:

  • Revoke the App's access to your WHOOP data via your WHOOP account settings
  • Disable or delete the automation in Make.com
  • Request deletion of any stored credentials

8. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for personal adult use only and does not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time. As this is a personal-use application, changes are made at the owner's discretion and do not require external notification.
